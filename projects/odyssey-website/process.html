Our Process

How We Build.

From your first call to the final walkthrough — a clear, step-by-step process with no surprises along the way.

Remodeling

How a Remodel Works

1

Free Consultation

We visit your home, listen to your goals, and walk the space together. No pressure, no hard sell — just an honest conversation about what's possible and what it takes. You don't need plans, blueprints, or a firm budget to get started.

2

Design & Scope

We develop a detailed scope of work that captures exactly what's changing, what materials are going in, and what the finished space will look like. For larger projects, we produce full design drawings. You'll know exactly what you're getting before anything is signed.

3

Transparent Estimate

You receive an itemized, line-by-line estimate — not a ballpark number. We walk you through every cost so you can make informed decisions about where to invest and where to value-engineer. No hidden fees, no surprises after the contract is signed.

4

Permits & Pre-Construction

We pull all required permits, order materials, and schedule trades in advance so your project starts on time. You'll receive a written project schedule with key milestones before demo begins.

5

Construction

Our crews work clean, organized jobsites. You'll have a dedicated project manager as your single point of contact — always reachable, always accountable. We send regular updates so you're never left wondering where things stand.

6

Final Walkthrough & Warranty

Before we consider a project closed, we do a detailed walkthrough with you to confirm everything meets our standards and yours. Any punch-list items are resolved quickly. Your warranty terms are documented and in writing.

New Construction

How a Custom Build Works

1

Initial Consultation & Site Review

We meet to understand your vision — size, style, must-haves, and budget range. If you already have a lot, we evaluate it for suitability. If you're still looking, we can advise on what makes a lot buildable and connect you with local real estate professionals.

2

Design & Architectural Planning

We develop full architectural drawings and 3D renderings so you can see the home before a shovel hits the ground. This is where selections happen — floor plans, elevations, structural details, and finish levels. Getting design right here saves time and money during the build.

3

Budget & Contract

You receive a transparent, itemized budget built around your actual plans — not a cost-per-square-foot guess. We review every line together before you commit to anything. Payment milestones are tied to construction phases, not arbitrary dates.

4

Permitting & Pre-Construction

We handle all permit applications, engineering coordination, and utility setup. We won't break ground until everything is approved and in order. You receive a full project schedule before construction begins.

5

Construction & Regular Updates

Your dedicated project manager oversees every phase — foundation, framing, mechanical, insulation, drywall, finishes. We schedule regular walkthroughs with you throughout the build so you can see progress and make real-time decisions on details before they're locked in.

6

Final Inspection & Key Handover

We complete a thorough punch list before your final inspection and certificate of occupancy. Once all inspections are passed, we do a full walkthrough to demonstrate systems, review warranties, and hand over keys to your finished home.
