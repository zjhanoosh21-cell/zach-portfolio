Platform Features

Everything a legal recruiter needs

Built from scratch for the CRI team — no bloated SaaS, no seat fees, no compromises on workflow.

👤

Candidate Profiles

Full candidate records with resume uploads, notes, follow-up dates, activity history, attachments, and status tracking through every stage.

💼

Job Orders

Create and manage open positions, assign recruiters, track submissions, and move candidates through a visual pipeline board.

🤖

AI Resume Triage

n8n workflow monitors ZipRecruiter inbox, extracts resumes, scores candidates with OpenAI, and posts them directly into the CRM — automatically.

📊

Analytics

Pipeline analytics, revenue tracking, candidate velocity, and placement reporting across the full recruiting funnel.

🔔

Notifications & Discussions

In-app notifications, per-record discussion threads, and @mention support so the team stays aligned on every candidate and job.

⚙️

Admin Controls

User management, role-based access, deletion PINs, custom dashboard layouts, email templates, and full data export.
